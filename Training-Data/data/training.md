## Training Data

training.zip is uploaded to /research_jude/rgs01_jude/groups/babugrp/home/common/MolSnap-2025 [only accessible to SJ people] as it's 12 GB.
The csv has headers image_id, file_path, SMILES.

There are about 619906 SMILES, and a paired 384x384 .png in an /img/ subfolder. 

Structures contain: ~270K from https://lotus.naturalproducts.net/ and ~340K from Enamine's 4.7M REAL library of funky macrocycles (filtered for MW > 600 Da).
